Join Us in Making a Difference

Support Our CSR Initiatives

We are excited to launch a meaningful initiative that combines our commitment to Corporate Social Responsibility with the spirit of giving. Our upcoming donation drive aims to support two vital causes: providing meals to those in need and equipping underprivileged children with essential school supplies. Here’s how you can contribute and help us reach our goals.


Our Goals

  • Supporting Willing Hearts: This incredible charity prepares and provides meals to those in need. Your donations will help ensure that more people can enjoy nutritious meals.
  • Empowering Children Through Education: Our corporation’s initiative, Operation Backpack, provides 100 free backpacks. We will fill these backpacks with essential stationery and writing materials for needy children.

Donation Milestones

  • First Milestone: $1,000
    • For Willing Hearts: Purchase food staples to support their meal programs.
    • For Wishing Well: QWL2024 releases a matching sum of $1000 to fund stationery for 50 backpacks.
  • Second Milestone: $2,000
    • For Willing Hearts: Purchase a different set of food staples.
    • For Wishing Well: QWL2024 releases a matching sum of $1000 to fund stationery for an additional 50 backpacks.
  • Final Milestone: $3,000
    • For Willing Hearts: Purchase more variety of food staples.

The donation drive will commence now and end on 30 October, 2024.


How You Can Help

  • Donate Generously: Your contributions will directly impact the lives of those in need. Every dollar counts, and reaching each milestone will make a significant difference.
  • Volunteer Selflessly:
    • Willing Hearts Kitchen: Join us for a day of volunteering at the Willing Hearts kitchen, where we’ll help prepare meals for the needy.
    • Backpack Packing Event: Help us pack the stationery into the backpacks and distribute them to the children.
