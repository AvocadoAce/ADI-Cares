Children’s Wishing Well was originally named as Student Advisory Centre when it was founded in 2002. When our CEO, Joanna (left), took over the helm in 2018, she renamed it Children’s Wishing Well.

The morale behind Children’s Wishing Well is three-fold:
A child's wish come true.
Wishing our children well.
A well of skills and values.


Go to Children's Wishing Well Homepage
Providing holistic education, assistance programmes, and opportunities so as to maximise every child and youth’s strengths.
Opportunities for every child and youth to succeed in life, regardless of their backgrounds.

What We Do?

We are dedicated to focusing on the holistic education of children and youth from disadvantaged backgrounds

Children Enrichment Programme

An all-encompassing program to develop children’s academic, life, and social skills.

Reach For The Star

Sponsoring talented students for training to excel in arts and sports.

F R E S H

An empowering program teaching children budgeting, nutrition and decision-making skills.

Career GPS

Exposing students to careers and motivating them to achieve career goals.

Grant-A-Wish

Allows students to request needed items not covered by government funds.

Daily Lunch

Providing nutritious hot lunches to ensure no child goes hungry.

Birthday Celebrations

Celebrating children’s birthdays with food, gifts, and joyful memories.

Transport Subsidy

Covering MOE FAS transport costs to prevent school absences.
