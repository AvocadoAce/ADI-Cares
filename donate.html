Goal #1 - $1000 Milestone

Our first fundraising milestone of $1000 will have a significant impact on two causes:

  • For Willing Hearts: Your generous contribution will help us purchase essential food staples, crucial for sustaining their meal programs. These staples will include bee-hoon\rice, canola\sunflower oil, macaroni, plain flour, coconut cream and 3-in-1 coffee. These will be used to prepare nutritious meals for those facing food insecurity. With your support, Willing Hearts can continue their vital work, providing comfort and nourishment to families and individuals in need, and making a tangible difference in their lives.

  • For Children's Wishing Well: In addition to our initial $1,000 target, we are thrilled to share that QWL will match this amount, bringing an extra $1,000 to the table. This matching grant will be allocated to purchasing stationery supplies for 50 backpacks received from Corporate. These backpacks will be filled with essential school supplies, such as notebooks, pencils, and other educational tools, ensuring that children from underprivileged backgrounds are well-prepared for their academic journey. This support not only helps in providing the materials needed but also empowers these students to excel in their education and build a brighter future.

    Together, we can achieve this milestone and make a profound impact on our community. Thank you for your support!

    Goal #2 - $2000 Milestone

    We’re thrilled to set our sights on the second milestone of $2,000, a goal that will further enhance our support for two incredible initiatives:

  • For Willing Hearts: With this milestone, we aim to provide an additional set of essential food staples to support their ongoing meal programs. This contribution will help replenish their supplies, ensuring they can continue to offer nourishing meals to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity. Every item purchased — from Canned Vegetarian, Mock duck, chicken, abalone, char siew — will directly benefit those in need, helping Willing Hearts to sustain their critical services and spread hope throughout the community.

  • For Children's Wishing Well: We are equally excited to announce that QWL will again match the amount raised, adding an extra $1,000 to the cause. This matched funding will go towards assembling another 50 backpacks filled with vital stationery supplies. These backpacks will be distributed to more students who require support for their educational endeavors. Each backpack will include essential items such as notebooks, pencils, erasers, and other school materials, empowering these children to fully engage in their learning and achieve their academic goals.

    Reaching this milestone will make a substantial difference, and every contribution brings us closer to our goal. Thank you for your unwavering support and dedication to making a positive impact in our community!

    • Goal #3 - $3000 Milestone

    As we reach the climax of our fundraising journey, we set our sights on our final milestone of $3,000. Achieving this goal will allow us to make an even greater impact in our community by supporting the important work of Willing Hearts:

  • For Willing Hearts: With this final milestone, we aim to purchase an additional set of essential food staples to further support their crucial meal programs. These staples — such as Dried mushroom, shrimp, frozen chicken, fish, vegetable prawn, chicken nuggets — will be used to prepare nutritious meals for those in need. By reaching this milestone, we help ensure that Willing Hearts can continue to provide vital sustenance to families and individuals facing food insecurity, offering them not just meals but also a sense of hope and security.

    Every dollar raised brings us closer to this goal and helps us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of many. Your support is invaluable, and together, we can achieve this milestone and continue to foster positive change in our community. Thank you for your generosity and commitment to this important cause!
